Good to Know

Common Questions

↔ Can we reassign seats?

Yes — but only before a seat holder completes their course. Once a learner finishes and receives their certificate, that seat is consumed. Each package grants a fixed number of certified graduates: 12, 24, or 48. This ensures every certification carries real value.

🎓 Do learners get a certificate?

Yes. Each learner receives a certificate upon completing their chosen course, issued by Kaizen Academy Australia — a recognised name in continuous improvement training.

📚 Can different people do different courses?

That's the whole point. One person might do TPS Fundamentals, another Value Stream Mapping, another Problem Solving — whatever fits their role. Each seat is independent.

💬 Need a larger team or custom quote?

If you need more than 48 seats or want a tailored package for your organisation, reach out directly. We're happy to work with you on a custom arrangement.
